ID:7405613973368246272 DAY 3/60 : Why Your Model Dies in Production You trained a model on your laptop. 98% accuracy. Perfect. Deploy to production → instantly crashes. Why? Different Python version, different NumPy, missing system libraries. Your environment ≠ production environment. Docker fixes this: It bundles your exact Python version, dependencies, system libraries, and model code into one container that runs identically everywhere — laptop → staging → production → cloud. 73% of ML deployment failures come from environment mismatches. Docker removes this problem completely. Tomorrow: Your first Dockerfile for ML models.
