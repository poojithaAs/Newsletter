No rollback. 10:32 : Traffic ramps. THE NUMBER WE DIDN'T DO • ~45,000 requests/sec • ~2–3 KB per log line • ≈ 90–130 MB/sec • ≈ 5–8 GB/min per region Multiply by pods. Multiply by regions. Multiply by autoscaling. At small scale, this is a log. At 50K RPS, it's infrastructure. #DAMAGE • Short outage window • Checkout failures • Seven-figure revenue impact • Unexpected logging bill • One very uncomfortable postmortem THE #FIX ✅ Log sampling enforced at 1% in prod ✅ PR checklist: "log volume at 100% traffic?" ✅ Disk alerts fire earlier ✅ Cost-impact alerts now escalate ✅ Fewer alerts, higher signal THE #LESSON This wasn't a logging bug. It was a scale + toil problem. Reliability isn't about stopping mistakes. It's about making normal decisions safe at scale. WHOSE #FAULT? Sara Baqla wrote the logs. Poojitha A S approved the PR.
