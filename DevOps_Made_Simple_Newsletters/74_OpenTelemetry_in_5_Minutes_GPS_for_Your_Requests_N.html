"It's slow" isn't a diagnosis. A trace, which shows the path of your request, is.

Shipping a package gives you a tracking number to see its full journey. OpenTelemetry does the same for software requests:

  • Tracks a request across your system

  • Shows where slowdowns or errors happened

  • Helps fix problems with facts, not guesses

✅ What You Get

  • Find where requests are slow from start to finish.

  • Fix live issues faster using a trace ID.

  • Measure how fast your AI tools are and how much they cost for each feature.
