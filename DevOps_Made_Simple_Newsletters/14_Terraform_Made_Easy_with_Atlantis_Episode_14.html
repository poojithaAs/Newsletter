🤝 Questions You Might Be Thinking

Q1: Do I need a CI/CD pipeline to use Atlantis? A: Nope! Atlantis works outside of CI/CD and directly from your PRs.

Q2: Can I use Atlantis with private GitHub repos? A: Yes, just make sure your token has the right scopes.

Q3: What happens if someone comments accidentally? A: You can restrict access in to approved users only.

Q4: Will multiple users break Terraform state? A: No, Atlantis uses locking to prevent that.

Q5: Is ngrok production-safe? A: No. Use HTTPS and DNS for prod setups.

Q6: Can I run Atlantis on Kubernetes? A: Yes! There's a Helm chart for that.

Q7: What if Atlantis crashes? A: Logs will help. Fix config and restart.

Q8: Is Atlantis better than Terraform Cloud? A: If you want full control and self-hosting: Yes.

Q9: Can Atlantis handle modules? A: Yep, just organize them well.

Q10: Can I use Slack with it? A: Not directly, but you can integrate with webhooks or scripts.

Atlantis doesn't just automate Terraform. It brings accountability, security, and peace of mind to infra changes.
