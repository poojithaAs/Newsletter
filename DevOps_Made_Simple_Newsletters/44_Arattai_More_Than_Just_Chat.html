Arattai: More Than Just Chat

View on LinkedIn

Redefining Digital Communication with Unwavering Privacy and User Control

✍️ By Poojitha A S | #Tech #Product #AI #Governance

🌍 The Landscape Today

In a world where every digital interaction leaves a trace, the quest for private and dependable communication is more urgent than ever.

"Arattai," which simply means chat in Tamil, isn't just another messaging app—it's a bold statement. It promises conversations that are easy, private, and trustworthy, backed by a philosophy that stands apart from the mainstream.

🏢 The Zoho Story: A Different Path

Arattai is born from the ethos of Zoho Corp, a company that has consistently chosen principles over quick wins.

As Sridhar Vembu, Zoho's co-founder, often emphasizes:

"We don't rely on ads or tracking. We always focus on earning customer trust."

Zoho's guiding principles:

  • 🚫 No quick fixes or chasing fads

  • 🔒 Privacy first—no ads, no tracking

  • 🤝 Trust as the ultimate currency

Arattai is the natural extension of this philosophy.

🔑 Why Arattai Matters Now

With giants like WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram, and Slack, does the world need another chat app?

Arattai believes yes—because it addresses what others only promise:

  • Trust → True end-to-end encryption. No hidden agendas.

  • Control → Share files, collaborate, integrate with tools—all while keeping ownership of your data.

  • Simplicity → Lightweight design, no clutter, no distractions.

📌 Think about it: A sales team sharing sensitive contracts, or a family exchanging private photos. With Arattai, you get speed and privacy—without compromise.

🔐 The True Meaning of Privacy

Many platforms say they care about privacy. Few build their entire business model around it. Zoho is that rare exception.

Zoho's commitment to users:

  • ❌ No selling of personal data

  • ⏳ No sacrificing trust for growth

  • 🏗️ Products built for longevity and reliability

Arattai is built on this very DNA.

💡 Lessons for Innovators

  • Names matter → "Arattai" feels natural, like a friendly conversation.

  • Trust retains users → People join apps for convenience, but they stay for trust.

  • Independence fuels clarity → Without ad revenue pressure, innovation stays user-first.

📝 TL;DR: Arattai's Core

  • Built on Trust → Ethical foundation, no compromise.

  • User Control → You own your digital interactions.

  • Privacy Always → Not a feature, but a promise.

Arattai shows that sometimes, a simple word like chat can carry a revolutionary idea.
