MCP&#x27;s 5 Foundational Features – What They Mean in Practice Roots – Think of these as the "safe zones" your AI can access. Defining roots ensures AI can&#x27;t wander into sensitive files or systems, even by accident. Sampling – Allows controlled, limited access to certain capabilities so you can test or fine-tune without giving full permissions right away.

Prompt – Standardizes instructions so outputs are predictable and repeatable, making it easier to debug or scale AI workflows. Resources – Lets you securely share files, APIs, or datasets in a read-only way—perfect for reference materials without risking edits or deletions. Tools – Gives AI specific capabilities (e.g., run a script, call an API) but only within the guardrails you define.
