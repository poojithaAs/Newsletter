#Day124 #PromQL PromQL, the powerful query language of Prometheus, is the backbone of monitoring, alerting, and troubleshooting in modern systems.

Whether you&#x27;re a beginner just starting your journey or an experienced professional, mastering PromQL is essential for: ✅ Building insightful Grafana dashboards ✅ Setting precise alerts for system reliability ✅ Debugging issues with real-time metrics ✅ Gaining deep visibility into your infrastructure Learning Path Highlights 1️⃣ Understand metrics, labels, and time series – the building blocks of Prometheus. 2️⃣ Learn basic and advanced queries like rate, sum, and avg_over_time. 3️⃣ Build interactive Grafana dashboards for CPU usage, memory, and more. 4️⃣ Use recording rules to precompute queries and alerting rules to monitor issues. 5️⃣ Troubleshoot efficiently with PromQL filters, range vectors, and visualizations. #ProTip: Start with simple queries, add label filters, and gradually explore aggregation and grouping. Practice is key to mastering PromQL! #DevOps #PromQL #Grafana #Prometheus
