#WeBrokeItTogether RAG Series Episode 021926 : The Wiki Wars The Setup: The company Wiki is a mess. It has a "2020 Remote Policy" (Strictly Forbidden) and a "2024 Remote Policy" (Allowed). When users ask "Can I work from home?", the bot panics and says: "Remote work is strictly allowed but forbidden."

Sara Baqla (Dev): "I&#x27;m adding a system prompt: &#x27;Ignore documents older than 2022.&#x27;"

Poojitha A S (DevOps): "That won&#x27;t work. The vector search doesn&#x27;t always know the date if it&#x27;s not in the metadata. It just sees text."

Sara Baqla: "Then I&#x27;ll tell the LLM to &#x27;Use common sense&#x27;."

Poojitha A S: "Sara, the LLM is reading two contradictory truths with equal confidence. It&#x27;s not lacking common sense, it&#x27;s lacking a clean source."

Sara Baqla: "So... I have to read the Wiki?"

Poojitha A S: "We have to clean the Wiki."

Fix: Poojitha wrote a script to archive any Confluence page not updated in the last 2 years. They De-noised the Database.

Result: With the contradicting "2020 Policy" removed from the search index, the bot immediately answered correctly with the 2024 policy. RAG Failure Point #4: Not Extracted. Garbage In, Garbage Out. If your database contains contradictory information, no amount of prompt engineering will fix the confusion. Clean your data.
