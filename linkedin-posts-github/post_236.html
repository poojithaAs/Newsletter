#DAY195 AI Series: Before ChatGPT — Part 4 AI x Observability: The Rise of the Self-Healing Stack Before AI could talk, it learned to listen—deeply. And when it mastered observability, it didn&#x27;t just detect issues. It started fixing them. Welcome to the age of the self-healing stack—where AI closes the loop between detection and action.

Here&#x27;s how it&#x27;s reshaping modern operations:

1. Observability Meets Intelligence Old school: Dashboards full of logs, metrics, and traces New era: AI stitches them into a real-time storyline Tools like:

• Chronosphere, Grafana Cloud with ML plugins

• AppDynamics + Cisco AI Ops They map relationships, detect anomalies, and predict impact—before you even see a graph spike.

2. From Alerts to Auto-Remediation It&#x27;s no longer "you get an alert, then act." Now:

• Incidents are classified

• Root causes are predicted

• Resolutions are triggered autonomously Example:

• PagerDuty AIOps + Runbook automation

• If service latency > X and DB CPU > Y → Rollback, scale up, or restart automatically Outcome? Fixes that happen while your team is asleep.

3. Dynamic Baselines and Behavioral Learning No more static thresholds. AI learns the normal behavior of each service—and flags deviations with precision. No noise, just signal. Tools that do this well:

• Dynatrace Davis, New Relic AI, DataDog Watchdog They evolve with your system. Just like your codebase does.
