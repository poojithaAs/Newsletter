Ever had one of those days that makes you question everything? I get it. You crush an interview but hear nothing back. Or you have one that just… didn&#x27;t go well. Here are 5 lessons that help me through days like that: Lesson 1: Take bad days and bad interviews as lessons, not hits to the heart. Ask, "What can I learn to make this better next time?" And if it still happens, that&#x27;s okay.

Rejection is just redirection. Lesson 2: Consistency, no matter what. Feel like doing it? Do it. Don&#x27;t feel like doing it? Still do it. Feelings aren&#x27;t facts. Lesson 3: Hello, good day! I&#x27;m glad you&#x27;re here. Hello, bad day—I see you. Treat good and bad days like relatives you didn&#x27;t choose but still gotta respect. Lesson 4: Faith and prayer matter.

It&#x27;s easier said than done, but faith can move mountains. Trust the universe, especially on the hardest days. Lesson 5: No one is perfect. Not the person you admire, not the one who doubts you, and definitely not the one who wrote this.
