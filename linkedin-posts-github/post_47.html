Episode 8 #WeBrokeItTogether The Bug We Couldn&#x27;t See (Monday, 10:15 AM. A support ticket appears.) SUPPORT TICKET: "Checkout taking 30+ seconds randomly."

Sara Baqla: "Checkout latency. Again."

Poojitha A S: "Do we have OpenTelemetry traces for it?"

Sara Baqla: "I found a trace. Eight hundred milliseconds total."

Poojitha A S: "But users are seeing thirty seconds."

Sara Baqla: "Then we&#x27;re not looking at the same request."

Poojitha A S: "What is the sampling rate?"

Sara Baqla: "Tail-based sampling. We only keep one out of every hundred requests."

Poojitha A S: "So we&#x27;re throwing away ninety-nine percent of the evidence."

Sara Baqla: "To save money."

Poojitha A S: "Including the slow ones."

Poojitha A S: "Let&#x27;s capture everything for ten minutes."

Sara Baqla: "That&#x27;s… a lot of data. 500GB of traces/hour"

Poojitha A S: "Hope our Jaeger backend can handle it.." (Three minutes later. Alarms.) JAEGER: "Out of memory. Queries timing out."

Sara Baqla: "The tracing system just fell over."

Poojitha A S: "Okay. Back to one percent."

Sara Baqla: "I&#x27;m checking payment service logs directly."

Poojitha A S: "Without traces to connect everything?"

Sara Baqla: "The good old-fashioned way."

Poojitha A S: "Payment requests are timing out at thirty seconds."

Sara Baqla: "Only on Visa transactions?"

Poojitha A S: "Visa&#x27;s API is slow today."

Sara Baqla: "And we couldn&#x27;t see it in traces. Let&#x27;s change how we sample Switching to head-based sampling."

Poojitha A S: "Always sample slow requests and errors."

Sara Baqla: "Capture everything over five seconds."

Poojitha A S: "And just 1% of the fast ones." TRACE VIEW: checkout → payment service → Visa gateway (28.4s)

Sara Baqla: "There it is."

Poojitha A S: "Head-based sampling caught it!."

Sara Baqla: "Capturing everything shows every problem."

Poojitha A S: "But it crashes the system."

Sara Baqla: "Capturing 1% saves money."

Poojitha A S: "But misses the real issues."

Sara Baqla: "So we sample intelligently."

Poojitha A S: "We watch what matters." #fullStackDeveloper #devOps #sre
