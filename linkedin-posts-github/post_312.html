The team is ready to log off and embrace the weekend. But first, one last Terraform deployment. Just a small infrastructure update. Nothing major. So it saw the "new" VPC, decided the old one was unused, and deleted it—along with all of its dependencies. And just like that, our entire cloud infrastructure evaporated. The Fallout Production down.
