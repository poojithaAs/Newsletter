Day 1 at #DataSecAI25 by Cyera set the tone in the best possible way. Spent the afternoon exploring some truly impressive sponsor demos across data and AI — so many creative approaches to security and automation in one place. I also got my copy of "Rise of the Machines: A Project Zero Trust Story" by George Finney personally signed.

A small moment, but one that felt special — especially with the message "Every step matters." The evening brought a completely different kind of learning: cowboy outfits, live music, and even a station where we mixed our own BBQ spice blend. Light-hearted, fun, and surprisingly memorable.

And it reminded me of something important: When you&#x27;re in a room with the right people, the way you learn changes. Your curiosity sharpens, your thinking expands, and you walk out more inspired than you walked in. Day 1 delivered exactly that. Ready for Day 2.
