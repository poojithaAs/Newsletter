#DAY198 AI Series: Before ChatGPT — Part 6 The AI Stack: How AI Transformed Infrastructure, Monitoring & Security — because DevOps didn&#x27;t just get automated… it got intelligent. DevOps didn&#x27;t just evolve with automation—it leveled up with intelligence. Here&#x27;s how the AI Stack changed everything:

1. Infrastructure: From Scripts to Smart Scaling Gone are the days of static provisioning.

• AI now predicts demand, right-sizes cloud resources, and auto-heals failing nodes.

• Tools like HashiCorp Nomad with ML plugins, AWS Auto Scaling with predictive insights, and Kubernetes + KEDA enable responsive, cost-efficient infra.

2. Monitoring: From Alerts to Anomaly Detection We don&#x27;t chase red alerts anymore—we catch what&#x27;s weird before it breaks.

• AI-powered observability tools like Dynatrace New Relic AI, and promethus AI + Cortex + ML detect patterns, forecast outages, and learn from your stack&#x27;s behavior.

3. Security: From Static Rules to Self-Learning Defense AI watches what "normal" looks like—and flags everything else.

• Behavioral models in CrowdStrike Darktrace and Chainguard now protect pipelines, APIs, and codebases in real-time.

• It&#x27;s zero trust, but with 100 eyes open. The AI Stack isn&#x27;t just a set of tools. It&#x27;s a mindset shift: From manual response to machine intelligence. From watching to foreseeing.
