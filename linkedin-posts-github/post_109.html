Commit Your Code Conference – Day 1 Recap #CYC2025 September 25, 2025 So many great sessions today. I honestly wished I had Hermione&#x27;s time-turner to attend more than one at a time. From keynotes to deep dives, every track had something I wanted to be part of. The day opened with Danny Thompson and Matthew McDole keynotes which reminded us how leadership and community fuel innovation.

What tied the rest of the day together for me was Melisa Im chess metaphor: "Careers are like chess. Sometimes you move forward, sometimes diagonal, sometimes just one square. But every move counts." I saw that theme echoed throughout

• Kierra Dotson challenged us to lead AI responsibly

• Naveen Kumar Kotha showed how observability builds trust in intelligent systems

• Aly Ibrahim reminded us that AI will not work on itself and ownership must be shared

• Cedric Clyburn turned unstructured data into something usable

• David Strickland made modernization less about leaps and more about steady moves And between it all I reconnected with Sara Baqla, M.S. and many others. The hallway conversations were just as powerful as the talks. Day 1 Lesson Whether it is AI, cloud, or careers, transformation is a chessboard built move by move and step by step.
