OatStack v1.0 – Because the first production environment is YOU. As DevOps engineers, we obsess over uptime, performance, and clean code. But how often do we give our body the same level of care as our infrastructure? This bowl? It&#x27;s not just breakfast—it&#x27;s a high-availability, low-latency deployment designed to keep my mind sharp and my system stable.

What&#x27;s inside this GutOps deployment?

• Oats = Terraform Infrastructure as breakfast. Lays the foundation. Soaks in complexity like a pro. Predictable, reusable, and sets the tone for the whole stack.

• Frozen berries = Prometheus + Grafana Always watching. Loaded with data (antioxidants). Sends alerts the moment energy levels dip.

• Nut & seed powder = Bash scripts May look messy, but this mix is loaded with logic. Raw power, hidden functionality—just like your favorite one-liner.

• A2 milk = CI/CD pipeline Blends it all together and ensures smooth delivery—without any breaking changes.

• No added sugar = Zero Trust No unknown inputs. No sneaky crashes. Only verified, clean ingredients make it past my firewall. Macros:

• 20g protein

• 10g fiber

• ~450 kcal

• 0 synthetic junk Because just like in DevOps, the quality of inputs determines the stability of your system.
