From Stage to Coffee to Story On August 19th, I spoke at the Dallas Software Developers Group Meetup – A Night of Cloud on AI as a Service. After the talk, Sara Baqla, M.S. reached out asking me for a a virtual coffee chat and I immediately said yes. I did my homework before we met, her journey from teaching music and robotics to becoming a full-stack developer is incredible.

Over coffee, small talk turned into a two-sided interview. Sara shared how saying "yes" opened doors in her career: "You don&#x27;t know what doors that could open." We spoke about fundamentals, DevOps, resilience, and how communities like 100Devs gave her the support of like-minded developers while she transitioned into tech.

That conversation is now becoming a special Interview Edition of my newsletter, DevOps Made Simple. Publishing this Friday at 8:30 AM CST. You&#x27;ll hear Sara Baqla, M.S. story in her own words, her challenges, her wins, and her passion for building technology with impact.
