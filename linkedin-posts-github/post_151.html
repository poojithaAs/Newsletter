Monitoring war: Zabbix vs Zoho OpsManager Which one actually gives you full visibility metrics, logs, traces, and Kubernetes? If you&#x27;re a DevOps engineer or SRE, you&#x27;ve probably asked this at some point. Here&#x27;s a clear, simple comparison to help you choose Zabbix

• ✅ Open-source and free

• ✅ Monitors servers, networks, apps, and Kubernetes (with Prometheus)

• ✅ Highly customizable dashboards and alerts But…

• ⚠️ No built-in tracing

• ⚠️ Needs external tools for log management

• ⚠️ Setup takes time and manual effort Best for: Teams that want full control and are comfortable with DIY setup Zoho OpsManager

• ✅ Cloud-based and ready to go

• ✅ Built-in support for metrics, logs, and APM tracing

• ✅ Auto-discovers Kubernetes resources (nodes, pods, services)

• ✅ Prebuilt dashboards and alerts But…

• ⚠️ Paid (with free trial)

• ⚠️ Less customizable than Zabbix for edge cases Best for: Teams that want fast, all-in-one monitoring without infrastructure overhead
