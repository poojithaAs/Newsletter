1. Kubernetes isn&#x27;t just orchestration anymore—it&#x27;s the AI backbone. We explored how platforms like ChatGPT operate across 65,000+ GPU nodes, all orchestrated by Kubernetes. 2. AI Pilots vs. AI in Production: Why Most Projects Fail One panelist shared how companies run into walls when scaling:

• They start with clean pilot data.

• But scaling brings in messy, inconsistent, siloed data.

• Migrations, acquisitions, and legacy systems make it worse. "It&#x27;s not about the model. It&#x27;s about whether your data is actually clean, normalized, and trustworthy."

3. Partner with your data governance team early. If you want AI to work in real business environments, team up with the people cleaning and validating your data. AI fails when data hygiene is skipped.
