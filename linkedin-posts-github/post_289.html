#DAY158 WSO2 https://lnkd.in/gVJbTUDJ #choreo Going Platformless in a World of Platforms – What Does It Mean? Another insightful session at #Kubernetes and Cloud Native North Dallas, with Lakmal Warusawithana presenting on Going Platform-less in the Era of Building Platforms. What is Platformless? Traditionally, PaaS abstracts infrastructure so developers can focus on coding.

But Choreo is taking it a step further—eliminating the concept of fixed platforms and making infrastructure truly invisible. How is Platformless Different from PaaS? PaaS Provides a managed environment to run applications (e.g., OpenShift, Cloud Foundry). Platformless Removes the need to think about clusters, VMs, or even Kubernetes.

You write code, and it runs anywhere without worrying about infrastructure. Why Does This Matter? With Choreo&#x27;s platformless approach: No DevOps overhead – No infra setup, auto-scaling built-in. Cloud-agnostic – Works across AWS, Azure, and GCP. Faster deployment – Developers can focus purely on writing code. Event-driven & AI-powered – Future-ready for modern workloads.

Does This Mean the End of Kubernetes? Not quite. Kubernetes is still the foundation, but tools like Choreo are making it fade into the background, allowing developers to focus on building applications rather than managing clusters. The Big Debate: Platform vs. Platformless? Is platformless the future, or will Kubernetes-driven PaaS continue to dominate?
