A Night of Cloud – Dallas Software Developers Group Meetup Tonight, I had the honor of sharing the stage with three incredible speakers at the Dallas Developers Meetup. Huge thanks to Danny Thompson for organizing and to Yum! Brands and DotLabs for sponsoring and supporting this community.

Here&#x27;s how the night flowed: Rishab Kumar kicked us off with a powerful demo on Google Cloud Run, showing how fast and cost-effective serverless deployments can be. I(Poojitha A S )presented on AI as a Service (AIaaS) — what it is, why it matters, and how it saves companies time, money, and security while also raising challenges around governance and compliance.

Suresh A M dove into Data Governance, breaking it down with practical analogies (like renovating a house!) and showing why classification, policies, and compliance matter for every organization.

Noah Jenkins closed the evening with an insightful talk on Identity & Access Management (IAM) — highlighting risks like "permission creep" and how automation + zero trust can prevent million-dollar breaches. Big takeaway from the night: Cloud isn&#x27;t just about tools — it&#x27;s about making systems scalable, secure, and human-friendly.

Grateful to be part of this vibrant Dallas developer community, where knowledge flows freely and everyone walks away a little smarter than they came in. #DallasDevelopers #Cloud #AI #DataGovernance #IAM #DevOps #Community
