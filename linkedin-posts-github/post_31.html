#WeBrokeItTogether : This Week in Kubernetes Autoscaling (6 parts) #Part6/6: Early Abort + Caching The biggest wins weren&#x27;t flashy. They were API call optimizations.

Sara Baqla: "What&#x27;s early abort?"

Poojitha A S: "When AWS has no capacity, don&#x27;t wait minutes retrying. Detect fast. Fail over instantly."

Sara Baqla: "That sounds obvious."

Poojitha A S: "You only learn it when you hit real scale."

Sara Baqla: "And caching launch templates?"

Poojitha A S: "Autoscaler kept calling AWS for empty ASGs. They got throttled."

Sara Baqla: "So they cached templates?"

Poojitha A S: "Yep. Suddenly hundreds of ASGs became safe."

Sara Baqla: "Tiny optimizations."

Poojitha A S: "Tiny at small scale. Critical at Airbnb scale." What We Changed Early abort + cached launch templates = scale without API throttling. Never-Forget Lesson At scale, boring optimizations beat flashy features. Series Complete Final: 5% cloud spend ↓, 30% → 65% utilization ↑, 100+ → 40 clusters.
