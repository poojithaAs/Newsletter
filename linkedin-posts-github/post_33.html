#WeBrokeItTogether: This Week in Kubernetes Autoscaling (6 parts) #Part5/6: Custom gRPC Expander They made Cluster Autoscaler ask a service: "Which node should I launch?"

Sara Baqla: "How does a custom gRPC expander work?"

Poojitha A S: "Autoscaler sends a request: &#x27;I need a node—pick the best type.&#x27;"

Sara Baqla: "And your service responds?"

Poojitha A S: "Runs your business logic: Spot availability, pricing, capacity, AZ (Availability Zones) balance."

Sara Baqla: "So autoscaler outsources the decision?"

Poojitha A S: "Exactly. Autoscaler becomes the worker. Your service becomes the brain."

Sara Baqla: "What if the service is down?"

Poojitha A S: "Falls back to default logic. But it&#x27;s usually milliseconds."

Sara Baqla: "Did Airbnb open-source it?"

Poojitha A S: "Yes. Supported in Cluster Autoscaler v1.24.0+."

Sara Baqla: "And it saved money?"

Poojitha A S: "5% of total cloud spend immediately." What We #Changed Built a gRPC expander: separates scaling from business logic. Never-Forget Lesson Make infrastructure dumb. Put intelligence in a service you control. #Next: early abort + launch template caching.
