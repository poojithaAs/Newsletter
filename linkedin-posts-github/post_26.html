#WeBrokeItTogether RAG Series Episode 021826 : The Context Cliff The Setup: A user asks: "What are the compliance rules for European data?" The system correctly retrieves 50 documents, including the "GDPR Master Policy." But the bot replies: "I&#x27;m not sure." Sara is pulling her hair out because the document is right there in the logs.

Sara Baqla (Dev): "It retrieved the document! I see it in the logs! Why did it ignore it?"

Poojitha A S (DevOps): "Where in the list is it?"

Sara Baqla: "Rank #45."

Poojitha A S: "And what&#x27;s our context window size set to?"

Sara Baqla: "4,096 tokens. Standard."

Poojitha A S: "Sara... the LLM only read the first 10 documents. It fell off the cliff before it even got to #45. You can&#x27;t just stuff 50 files into a small box and hope it reads the bottom one."

Sara Baqla: "So we increase the context window to 100k?"

Poojitha A S: "And pay $5 per query? No. We change how we feed the data."

Fix: Poojitha changed the Consolidation Strategy. Instead of "Stuffing" (shoving everything in at once), she switched to "Refine" mode, where the LLM reads chunks in batches, summarizes them, and passes the summary to the next batch.

Result: The bot successfully synthesized the answer from Document #45 without blowing the token budget. RAG Failure Point #3: Not in Context. Retrieving a document doesn&#x27;t mean the LLM sees it. If your consolidation strategy is weak, your best data might be falling off the context cliff. #DevOps #SRE #RAG #SWE #VectorDatabase
