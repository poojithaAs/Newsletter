At C3, we believe learning is most powerful when done together. This week, we hosted an AI for Beginners workshop, led by Poojitha A S, who guided the community through the basics of AI in a simple, approachable way.

The session covered: What AI is and how it has evolved How machine learning works in three simple stages: training, learning, inference How ChatGPT was trained using supervised learning and human feedback Tokens & temperature—the building blocks and creativity dial of AI Practical ways AI can support daily life: resumes, study, planning, and creativity The heart of C3 remains the same: creating a safe, supportive space where women can ask, learn, and grow without judgment. This workshop was another step in turning curiosity into confidence. #C3Women #Community #AI #LearningTogether
