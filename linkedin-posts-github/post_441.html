##Scenario 2: Slow Playbooks #Performance #Ansible - Question: Why is your Ansible playbook taking too long? - Solution: Increase the number of forks to execute tasks concurrently. ##Scenario 3: Dynamic Inventory #Inventory #AWS #EC2 - Question: How do you manage hosts in AWS EC2? - Solution: Use the `aws_ec2` plugin or a dynamic inventory script.

##Scenario 4: Secret Management #Security #AnsibleVault - Question: How do you keep sensitive data safe in Ansible? - Solution: Use `ansible-vault` to encrypt sensitive files or variables.
