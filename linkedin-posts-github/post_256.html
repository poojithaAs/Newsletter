Had an incredible time at the Kubernetes and CloudNative North Dallas Meetup yesterday! It was an evening packed with learning, great conversations, and insights into the evolving world of Cloud Development Environments (CDEs).

Dave Ahr delivered a fantastic talk on "A Tale of Two Developers - An Introduction to Cloud Development Environments," breaking down how CDEs are transforming the way developers build, collaborate, and deploy in the cloud. It was fascinating to see how tools like Coder streamline the development process, making remote and cloud-based workflows more efficient.

Beyond the session, I had some amazing discussions with fellow tech enthusiasts about modern DevOps, Kubernetes, and cloud-native tooling. Events like this remind me why I love being part of such a dynamic and forward-thinking community! A huge thanks to our sponsors Improving and KubeWize Inc Inc, and of course, to Tommy Decker for organizing this amazing event.
